## How Purely White Deluxe Is Claimed to Work

Purely White Deluxe uses a blend of technologies and ingredients that work together:

- **PAP+ stain removal** technology helps break down surface stains without peroxide or harsh chemicals.
- **Hydroxyapatite** helps strengthen enamel while whitening, which may reduce sensitivity during and after use.
- **CPC (cetylpyridinium chloride)** aids breath freshening and antibacterial support as part of daily oral care.
- **LED light** claimed to accelerate whitening effects in short sessions, often around 7 minutes.

This combination aims to whiten and protect teeth with minimal irritation or discomfort compared with peroxide-based products.
